About Me

Design Thinking
Meets Visual Soul

I believe great design begins long before the screen — in observation, curiosity, and the quiet act of seeing the world differently. As a BFA student specialising in Graphic Design and Visual Communication, I bridge the gap between raw artistic instinct and structured design thinking.

My work spans hand-drawn illustration, watercolour, and digital design — each piece driven by a desire to communicate ideas that linger. I find equal joy in the texture of a pencil sketch and the precision of an Illustrator artboard. Every project is a story waiting for the right visual language.
